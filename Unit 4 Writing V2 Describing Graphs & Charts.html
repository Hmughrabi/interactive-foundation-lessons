📈 Understanding the Data

Rainfall in Tehran (mm)

Bar Chart: Monthly Rainfall

Highest: December (40mm)

Lowest: June (3mm)

Temperatures in Tehran (°C)

Line Graph: Monthly Temperatures

Hottest: July (40°C)

Coolest: January (10°C)

Writing Plan:

1
What? rainfall and temperatures
2
Where? in Tehran
3
When? over one year

✍️ Model Answer

The graphs show rainfall and temperatures in Tehran over a year.

First, the bar chart shows the average rainfall in millimeters. The highest rainfall is in December. It reaches 40 mm. December is the rainiest month. The lowest rainfall is in June and July. It falls to 2mm. June and July are the driest months. July is drier than August. November is rainier than September.

Finally, the line graph shows the average temperature in degrees Celsius. The maximum temperature is in July. It rises to 40 °C. The hottest month is July. The minimum temperature is in January, February and December. It drops to 10 °C. They are the warmest months. August is hotter than October. The temperature in March is as warm as that in November.

Overall, it seems that Tehran has extreme weather in July. There can be heatwaves. The best time to visit this city is in March and November because the weather is warm and rainy.

📝 Key Vocabulary

Rainfall - the amount of rain that falls
Temperatures - how hot or cold it is
Highest/Maximum - the most
Lowest/Minimum - the least
Wettest - with the most rain
Driest - with the least rain
Hottest - with the highest temperature
Coolest - with the lowest temperature
Reaches - arrives at, gets to
Falls to - goes down to

2. Choose the best sentence to compare two values:

July is hot and August is hot

Correct! "July is hotter than August" clearly compares the two months.
3. What should you include when describing data?

Just the lowest values

Correct! Good descriptions include specific numbers and make comparisons.
4. Which sentence correctly uses "reaches"?

The temperature reaches 40°C
Reaches the temperature high

Correct! "The temperature reaches 40°C" properly uses "reaches" with a specific value.
5. What is the purpose of the conclusion in a graph description?

To give an overall observation or recommendation
To introduce new information
To list all the months
Correct! The conclusion should provide an overall observation or recommendation based on the data.
